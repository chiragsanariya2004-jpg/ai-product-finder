Why Camera Quality Matters in Smartphones

For many users, the camera is the most important feature of a smartphone. People use their phones daily to capture memories, record videos, create social media content and even shoot professional looking photos.

Modern smartphones include advanced camera technology such as AI scene detection, night photography algorithms, optical stabilization and HDR processing.

These technologies allow users to capture stunning photos even without professional photography skills.

Key Camera Features To Look For

1. High Megapixel Camera

Most smartphones under ₹25000 now offer cameras between 50MP and 108MP. Higher megapixels allow the camera to capture more detail and improve image clarity.

2. Night Mode

Night photography has become a major feature in modern smartphones. AI based night mode allows the phone to capture brighter images in low light conditions.

3. Optical Image Stabilization

OIS helps reduce blur in photos and videos by stabilizing the camera sensor. This is especially useful when shooting videos or taking photos in low light.

4. 4K Video Recording

Many smartphones in this price range now support 4K video recording, making them great for vloggers and content creators.

5. AI Camera Processing

AI processing helps smartphones automatically adjust brightness, contrast and color balance to produce better images.
