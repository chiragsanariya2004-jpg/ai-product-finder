Things To Consider Before Buying a Phone Under 25000

1. Processor Performance

The processor is the most important component of a smartphone. If you want smooth performance, fast app loading and lag free gaming, you should choose a phone with a powerful processor.

Some of the best processors in this price range include Snapdragon 7 series processors and MediaTek Dimensity processors. These chipsets provide excellent performance for gaming, multitasking and everyday use.

2. Display Quality

Most smartphones under ₹25000 now offer high refresh rate displays such as 120Hz or 144Hz panels. These displays provide a smoother scrolling experience and make gaming more enjoyable.

You should also look for AMOLED displays because they offer better colors, deeper blacks and improved viewing angles compared to LCD screens.

3. Camera Performance

Camera quality is another important factor when buying a smartphone. Many smartphones under 25000 now come with 50MP or even 64MP cameras that deliver excellent photos in daylight conditions.

If photography is important to you, choose a phone with good image processing and optical stabilization.

4. Battery Life

Battery capacity plays a big role in daily smartphone usage. Most smartphones in this segment offer 5000mAh batteries which easily last a full day on moderate usage.

Fast charging is also important. Many phones now support 67W or even 80W fast charging which can charge the phone in less than an hour.
