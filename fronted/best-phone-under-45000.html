How to Choose the Best Phone Under 45000

1. Processor

The processor determines the overall performance of a smartphone. If you play games or use heavy apps, choosing a phone with a flagship chipset is important for long‑term performance.

2. Camera Performance

Many smartphones in this price segment offer high‑resolution sensors and advanced image processing. If photography is your priority, choose a phone with strong HDR and night photography features.

3. Display Quality

AMOLED displays with high refresh rates provide smoother scrolling, better color accuracy, and improved viewing angles.

4. Battery Life

Battery capacity between 4500mAh and 5500mAh is common in this segment. Fast charging technology is also important for quickly powering up your device during busy days.
