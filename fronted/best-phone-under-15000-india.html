The processor is the brain of your smartphone. If you want smooth performance and good gaming capability, you should choose a phone with a powerful processor. Popular processors in this price range include Snapdragon series, MediaTek Dimensity processors and Helio chipsets.

2. Battery Life

Most smartphones under ₹15000 come with 5000mAh batteries which can easily last a full day. If you use your phone for gaming, watching videos and social media, a large battery is very important.

3. Camera Quality

Camera performance has improved a lot in budget smartphones. Many phones now come with 50MP cameras, night mode, AI photography and portrait features.

4. Display Quality

Many phones now offer Full HD+ displays and even 90Hz or 120Hz refresh rate which makes scrolling and gaming smoother.
