What to Look for in a Phone Under 15000?

Before buying a budget smartphone, you should focus on these important factors:

  • Processor Performance: For smooth daily use and gaming, choose Snapdragon 4 Gen series or MediaTek Dimensity processors.
  • Battery Life: Minimum 5000mAh battery is recommended.
  • Display: 90Hz or 120Hz refresh rate gives smoother experience.
  • Camera: 50MP primary sensor is common in this price range.
  • 5G Support: Future-proof your purchase with 5G connectivity.
