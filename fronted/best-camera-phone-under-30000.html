Key Camera Features You Must Check

1. Sensor Size Matters More Than Megapixels

Many people focus only on megapixels, but sensor size plays a bigger role. A larger sensor captures more light, resulting in better dynamic range and improved low-light performance.

2. Optical Image Stabilization (OIS)

OIS reduces blur and shake while taking photos or recording videos. If you want sharp night photos and stable videos, OIS is extremely important.

3. Night Mode Performance

A good night mode uses AI and long exposure techniques to brighten dark scenes without too much noise.

4. Ultra-Wide Camera

For landscape and group photography, an ultra-wide lens is essential.

5. Video Recording Capabilities

If you create reels or YouTube videos, make sure the phone supports 4K recording with stabilization.
