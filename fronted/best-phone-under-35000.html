How to Choose the Best Phone Under 35000

Performance is one of the most important aspects of a smartphone. A powerful processor ensures smooth multitasking, better gaming performance, and faster app loading times.

Modern smartphones include advanced camera systems capable of capturing high‑quality photos in different lighting conditions. AI image processing also improves overall photography results.

AMOLED displays with high refresh rates offer vibrant colors, deep blacks, and smooth animations, making them ideal for gaming and multimedia consumption.
